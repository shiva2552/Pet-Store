Monsieur Teddy

Breed American Shorthair

Teddy has a very soft coat and he is very playful. He loves getting attention but there are times when he would go hide behide the closet or under the bed. Teddy is very smart and he is able to learn very quickly. He likes to drink alot of water and he doesn't like his food dry so make sure to mix in some wet food as well.
